    OptionalstepSeconds

    stepSeconds?: number

    Sampling step in seconds for the internal twilight-band scan.

    Lowering this slows construction but does not improve accuracy past 1 (the band-edge resolution is already ±stepSeconds seconds, so 1 is the precision floor). Raise it to e.g. 60 if you only need minute-level boundaries and want a much faster constructor.

    Default

    1
